The Research Support Facility (RSF), based at The Alan Turing Institute
with collaborators from Swansea University and the University of
Edinburgh, offers AI and advanced data science support to the research
teams part of the [NIHR&#39;s Artificial Intelligence for Multiple Long-Term Conditions (AIM)](https://www.nihr.ac.uk/blog/artificial-intelligence-to-understand-clusters-of-multiple-long-term-conditions-an-nihr-priority/25171) project, and acts as a hub to foster collaboration across the AIM consortia.

For more information about the RSF, please visit our webpage ([AI formultiple long-term conditions: Research Support Facility | The AlanTuring Institute](https://www.turing.ac.uk/research/research-projects/ai-multiple-long-term-conditions-research-support-facility)) or contact us direct at: [aimrsf@turing.ac.uk](mailto:aimrsf@turing.ac.uk).
